# 15 - Glossary (English + Hindi)

## Product Terms

- RideEasy: EV ecosystem platform for mobility, energy, and rewards.
- Eco-Points: sustainability reward points earned from rides and actions.
- Green Credits/Coins: redeemable reward value for partner ecosystem.
- Dual App Model: user app + rider/station operations app.

## Business Terms

- Subscription Plan: recurring monthly package with benefits.
- Pay-Per-Use: per transaction pricing without monthly commitment.
- Customer Portal: B2B dashboard for institutions and companies.
- Commission Split: payout distribution among partners and platform.

## Technical Terms

- RBAC: role-based access control.
- Ledger: append-only transaction record for wallet operations.
- Webhook: gateway callback for transaction status updates.
- Idempotency: duplicate request safe processing.

## EV and Operations Terms

- Battery Swap: replacing low battery with charged battery.
- Station Load: current usage pressure at charging station.
- Range Confidence: user trust that trip can complete with available energy.
- Energy Sharing Session: assisted charge/swap support event.

## Hindi Interpretations

- Mobility + Energy Platform: transport aur energy support ka combined system.
- Sustainability Rewarding: eco behavior par measurable incentives dena.
- Smart Ride Advisor: route aur battery decisions me AI-based guidance.
- Open EV Intelligence: station data aur usage insights ka structured network.
