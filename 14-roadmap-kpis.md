# 14 - Roadmap and KPI Framework

## Objective

Translate architecture and product vision into executable phases.

Roadmap ka purpose hai practical delivery milestones define karna jisse team predictable execution kar sake.

## Phase Plan

### Phase 0 - Foundation
- auth baseline
- role model
- map integration setup
- payment gateway sandbox integration
- basic booking flow

### Phase 1 - Core Commercial Launch
- individual onboarding complete
- ride lifecycle stable
- subscription + pay-per-use active
- eco-points wallet v1
- admin dashboard v1

### Phase 2 - B2B and Operational Scale
- customer portal and bulk onboarding
- organization billing workflows
- advanced analytics for admins and customers

### Phase 3 - Differentiator Rollout
- battery sharing pilot
- AI route/energy recommendation beta
- green credit partner marketplace pilot
- crowdsourced EV grid mapping

### Phase 4 - Ecosystem Expansion
- dual app synergy full operations
- multi-city station and partner scaling
- model optimization and policy hardening

## KPI Categories

1. Growth Metrics
- monthly active users
- new user conversion
- organization acquisition

2. Revenue Metrics
- MRR and ARR
- plan-wise revenue share
- pay-per-use contribution

3. Ride Metrics
- booking to completion ratio
- ETA accuracy
- cancellation rate

4. Energy Metrics
- average battery saving via AI advice
- successful battery support sessions
- charging failure reduction

5. Rewards Metrics
- eco-point earn and redemption rates
- green credit marketplace utilization

6. Reliability and Trust Metrics
- payment success rate
- incident frequency
- support resolution time

## Launch Readiness Checklist

- critical flows tested in staging
- monitoring and alerts active
- rollback plans documented
- customer support playbooks ready
- finance reconciliation verified
