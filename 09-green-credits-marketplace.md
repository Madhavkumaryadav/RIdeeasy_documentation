# 09 - Green Credits and Local Business Marketplace

## Objective

Create an ecosystem where sustainable rides generate redeemable value beyond ride discounts.

Green credit model ka objective hai app engagement ko local economy ke saath connect karna.

## Reward Units

- Eco-Points: in-platform behavior score and utility wallet.
- Green Credits/Coins: redeemable value in partner ecosystem.

## Earning Channels

- completed eco rides
- verified charging actions
- station contribution events
- community participation milestones

## Redemption Channels

- cafes and food partners
- EV accessory shops
- service centers
- online partner brands

## Partner Model

Partner types:
- local merchants
- EV ecosystem vendors
- digital commerce partners

Partner benefits:
- targeted green audience
- campaign visibility
- measurable redemption analytics

## Settlement Model

- periodic settlement cycle
- redemption verification event
- platform fee handling
- reconciliation and dispute support

## Fraud Controls

- one-time tokenized redemption
- merchant verification checks
- suspicious pattern alerts

## KPIs

- credit earning to redemption ratio
- partner retention rate
- average redemption value
- incremental rides influenced by credits
